Core Operational Programmes

Structured intervention frameworks driving public health protection, human rights clarity, and youth livelihood engineering.

Behavioral Health

Dandaro Youth Behavioral Forums

Our clinical and peer-led Dandaro sessions create structural safe spaces for over 200 regular adolescents. These platforms focus on comprehensive HIV/AIDS literacy, reproductive health rights, mental wellness, and anti-substance abuse matrices.

  • Structured weekly peer-to-peer tracking groups
  • Private mental health counseling access channels
Public Infrastructure

Youth Drop-In & Information Centre

Operating directly from the Chitungwiza Publicity Complex, our Walk-In Drop Centre offers safe asylum and access to tools for local youth. Featuring an active micro-resource library, the facility has recorded a +40% increase in safe-space usage.

  • Educational material library & reading resource rooms
  • Walk-in specialist consultation desks for youth guidance
Livelihoods

Vocational Chemical & Agro-Enterprise

To address poverty vectors that compound health vulnerabilities, we train youth in commercial liquid detergent manufacturing and sustainable greenhouse farming techniques. Graduates receive startup micro-packs to form legal micro-production networks.

Eco ChemicalFormulating hand-pressed soaps and high-grade detergents.
Smart FarmingGreenhouse urban techniques for localized food safety and income vectors.
